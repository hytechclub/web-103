Warm-Up: Top Ten Restaurants

Follow these instructions to build a top ten list of your favorite restaurants.

  1. Open the Top Ten Restaurants Website
  2. Delete any restaurants that are not in your top ten
  3. Edit existing restaurants to update their positions
  4. Add new restaurants (at least three) to the bottom of the list

By the end of the activity, your page should have ten total restaurants, ranked 1-10!
